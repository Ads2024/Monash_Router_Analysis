\author{Adam Mohamed}

\maketitle

The threshold length, \( L_{\text{threshold}} \), is defined as:
\begin{equation}
L_{\text{threshold}} = \frac{\text{Propagation delay difference}}{\text{Inverse bandwidth difference}}
\end{equation}
